Frequently Asked Questions

What are YouTube tags?

YouTube tags are keywords that help YouTube understand what your video is about. They improve video discoverability in search results.

How many tags should I use?

Most creators use 5–15 relevant tags that match the video topic and audience search queries.

Do tags help YouTube SEO?

Yes, tags help YouTube understand your content and can improve video rankings when combined with good titles and descriptions.
